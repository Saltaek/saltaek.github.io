# Privacy Policy for Idleventure

**Last updated:** June 2026

Thank you for playing **Idleventure**.

This Privacy Policy explains how Idleventure handles user data.

## 1. Data Collection

Idleventure is designed as a primarily offline single-player game.

The game **does not collect, store, transmit, or share personal data** such as:

* Full name
* Email address
* Phone number
* Location
* Payment information
* Login credentials

Idleventure does **not** require account creation or login.

## 2. Player Nickname

During onboarding, players may choose a nickname for gameplay purposes.

This nickname:

* is chosen locally by the player
* is stored only on the device
* is used only inside the game

This nickname is **not transmitted to external servers**.

## 3. Local Save Data

Idleventure stores game progress locally on the user’s device.

Examples of locally stored game data include:

* Player progress
* Experience and levels
* Inventory and equipment
* Talents and upgrades
* Pets and achievements
* Settings and preferences
* Daily reward state
* Selected nickname

This data is stored only to provide game functionality.

Idleventure currently uses local storage mechanisms such as:

* SharedPreferences
* DataStore
* Local database storage (Room)

Game data is **not uploaded to cloud services**.

## 4. Third-Party Services

Idleventure currently does **not** use third-party services for:

* Analytics
* Advertising
* Crash reporting
* User tracking

This means the game does **not** include services such as:

* Firebase Analytics
* Google AdMob
* Crashlytics

## 5. Ads and In-App Purchases

Idleventure currently includes:

* No advertisements
* No in-app purchases

## 6. Children’s Privacy

Idleventure is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Since no personal information is collected, there is no intentional collection of children’s data.

## 7. Data Security

Because Idleventure does not transmit personal data to external servers, user privacy risks are minimized.

All game progress is stored locally on the device.

Users are responsible for maintaining backups of their device data when applicable.

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future if game features change.

Examples include:

* cloud save
* online multiplayer
* analytics
* ads
* in-app purchases

Any updates will be reflected in a revised Privacy Policy.

## 9. Contact

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy or Idleventure, please contact the developer through the official store listing or official support channels.
