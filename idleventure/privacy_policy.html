Privacy Policy for Idleventure

Last updated: June 2026

Thank you for playing Idleventure.

This Privacy Policy explains how Idleventure handles user data.

1. Data Collection

Idleventure is a primarily offline single-player game.

The game does not collect, store, transmit, or share personal data such as:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location
  • Payment information
  • Login credentials

Idleventure does not require account creation or login.

2. Player Nickname

Players may choose a nickname during onboarding for gameplay purposes.

This nickname:

  • is chosen locally by the player
  • is stored only on the device
  • is used only inside the game

This nickname is not transmitted to external servers.

3. Local Save Data

Idleventure stores game progress locally on the user’s device.

Examples include:

  • Player progress
  • Experience and levels
  • Inventory and equipment
  • Talents and upgrades
  • Pets and achievements
  • Settings and preferences
  • Daily reward state
  • Selected nickname

Idleventure uses local storage mechanisms such as:

  • SharedPreferences
  • DataStore
  • Local database storage (Room)

Game data is not uploaded to cloud services.

Important: Since progress is currently stored only locally, uninstalling the application, clearing app data, or factory resetting the device may permanently delete game progress.

Players are responsible for preserving access to their device and local app data until cloud backup features are introduced.

4. Local Notifications

Idleventure may display local notifications on the user’s device, such as gameplay reminders, reward availability, or progression-related alerts.

These notifications are generated locally on the device using Android system notification features and do not require sending personal data to external servers.

Users can disable notifications at any time through their device settings.

5. Third-Party Services

Idleventure currently does not use third-party services for:

  • Analytics
  • Advertising
  • Crash reporting
  • User tracking
  • Remote push notifications

6. Future Online Features and Save Migration

Idleventure currently operates as an offline single-player experience.

Future updates may introduce optional online features such as:

  • Cloud save
  • Online multiplayer
  • Leaderboards
  • Competitive events

If such systems are introduced, existing local save data may require migration, adjustment, balancing, or separation from future online progression to ensure fair gameplay and prevent cheating.

The developer may apply progression balancing, migration systems, or compensation rewards when major gameplay systems are updated.

7. Ads and In-App Purchases

Idleventure currently includes:

  • No advertisements
  • No in-app purchases

8. Children’s Privacy

Idleventure is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. Data Security

Since Idleventure does not transmit personal data to external servers, privacy risks are minimized.

All progress is stored locally on the device.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This policy may be updated if future features include:

  • Cloud save
  • Online multiplayer
  • Analytics
  • Ads
  • In-app purchases
  • Remote push notifications
